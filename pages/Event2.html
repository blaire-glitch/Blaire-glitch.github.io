Community Evening of Worship

Event Details

Date: Friday, January 10, 2026

Time: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Venue: ACK St Thomas Cathedral, Busia Town

Entry: Free - Love Offering Welcome

About This Event

Join us for an intimate evening of praise, worship, and fellowship as we gather together to honor God and uplift our community through song and prayer.

The Community Evening of Worship is a special gathering designed to bring believers together for a refreshing time in God's presence. Experience powerful worship led by Arise Band KE, hear inspiring testimonies from community members, and enjoy a spirit-filled atmosphere of unity and celebration.

This event is perfect for families, youth, and anyone seeking spiritual renewal. Whether you're a long-time believer or exploring faith for the first time, you are warmly welcomed to join us for this memorable evening of worship.

Event Highlights

  • ✦ Live worship music by Arise Band KE
  • ✦ Inspirational testimonies and ministry
  • ✦ Community fellowship and networking
  • ✦ Prayer and spiritual encouragement
  • ✦ Light refreshments provided
