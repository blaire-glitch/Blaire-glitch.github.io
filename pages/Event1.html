Open Heaven Experience - Concert

Event Details

Date: Sunday, December 15, 2025

Time: 7:00 PM - 11:00 PM

Venue: Busia Stadium, Busia County

Entry: Free Admission - All Are Welcome

About This Event

Experience an unforgettable night of powerful worship, supernatural encounters, and divine presence at the Open Heaven Experience Concert.

The Open Heaven Experience is more than just a concert—it's a spiritual journey where heaven meets earth through worship and praise. Join thousands of believers for an electrifying night of live music, prophetic ministry, and transformative worship led by Arise Band KE and special guest artists.

Inspired by Romans 15:6 - "That together you may with one voice glorify the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ" - this event brings the community together in unity, faith, and celebration. Expect powerful testimonies, Spirit-filled worship, and miracles as we create an atmosphere for God's glory to manifest.

Whether you're seeking breakthrough, healing, or simply want to worship with fellow believers, this is your moment. Come expecting, come prepared to encounter God in a fresh way!

Event Highlights

  • ✦ Live worship concert by Arise Band KE
  • ✦ Special guest ministries and worship leaders
  • ✦ Powerful testimonies and prophetic declarations
  • ✦ Prayer for healing and breakthrough
  • ✦ Community fellowship and networking
  • ✦ Surprise performances and giveaways
