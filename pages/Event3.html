Youth Praise Concert

Event Details

Date: Friday, December 27, 2025

Time: 5:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Nambale Grounds, Busia County

Entry: Free - Youth Focused Event

About This Event

Calling all young people! Join us for an explosive evening of praise, worship, and youth empowerment at the Youth Praise Concert.

The Youth Praise Concert is specifically designed for young believers, students, and youth groups to come together and experience God in a dynamic, relevant, and exciting way. This is not your average church service—it's a high-energy celebration of faith, featuring contemporary worship, powerful messages, and interactive sessions tailored for the youth.

Led by Arise Band KE alongside emerging young worship leaders, this event creates a platform where young voices are heard, talents are celebrated, and faith is ignited. Expect electrifying worship, inspiring testimonies from young believers, motivational talks about purpose and destiny, and opportunities to connect with peers who share your passion for Christ.

Whether you're a seasoned believer or exploring faith for the first time, this event is your space to encounter God, discover your purpose, and be empowered to make a difference in your generation. Bring your friends, bring your energy, and get ready for an unforgettable experience!

Event Highlights

  • ✦ Contemporary worship and praise by young artists
  • ✦ Youth testimonies and inspiring stories
  • ✦ Motivational talks on purpose and calling
  • ✦ Interactive sessions and youth activities
  • ✦ Networking with like-minded young believers
  • ✦ Talent showcase and competitions
  • ✦ Refreshments and hangout zones
